**Abstract:** 

In this fast-paced talk, Jesse will explain how to introduce DevOps to your Boss & talk about it inside your Business.   He will explain how to evangelize & overcome cultural resistance to change (& share his own painfully funny lessons on how not to do it ;-).  Jesse will teach you just enough Business Speak to be dangerous while you infect your organization with awesome, make your job better, and get more done.

**Speaker:** Jesse Robbins - Opscode
