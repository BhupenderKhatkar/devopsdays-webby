--- extension: html filter: - erb - markdown title: location layout: event dirty: true --- ### Conference location: Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre. Grey Street - Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre
South Brisbane, Brisbane Information on getting to the Venue, Parking and Public Transport. The venue is a short 10 minute walk from the CBD across the Brisbane River via the Victoria Bridge Bus - Regular bus services run via the Cultural Centre or South Brisbane Busway Stations (5 minute walk). Train - South Brisbane Railway Station is adjacent to the complex on Grey Street. Car Park A 1,500 vehicle paid car park is available at the centre. There are also nearby alternative paid car parks at South Bank and the Queensland Performing Arts Complex.
