--- extension: html filter: - erb - markdown title: Location dirty: true layout: event platinum: false --- The conference will be held at [The United States Patent and Trademark Office](http://www.uspto.gov/)'s Madison Auditorium at 600 Dulany Street, Alexandria, Virginia, just outside the District (but inside the Beltway!) The DevOpsDays DC team urges you to take [Metro](http://www.wmata.com/) to the event. USPTO is located a few blocks from the [King Street/Old Town](http://www.wmata.com/rail/station_detail.cfm?station_id=48) station, which is served by Blue and Yellow line trains, both VRE commuter lines, and Amtrak. It's also a short ride from [National Airport (DCA)](http://www.metwashairports.com/reagan/reagan.htm), our recommended airport for visitors from out of town. DC and Northern Virginia traffic is consistently rated among the worst in the nation, so we recommend that you do not drive, especially if you are visiting from out of town. If you must drive, please see [USPTO's directions page](http://www.uspto.gov/about/contacts/locations/directions.jsp).
